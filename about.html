SRITW is one of the leading institutions dedicated to empowering women through quality technical education. Established with a vision to promote innovation, research, and leadership among women engineers, SRITW provides an excellent academic environment supported by experienced faculty and modern infrastructure. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines and focuses on nurturing both technical and soft skills to prepare students for global opportunities. With a commitment to excellence, SRITW continues to inspire young women to achieve success in the field of science and technology.
